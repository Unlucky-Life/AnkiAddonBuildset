A modern, minimalistic music player addon for Anki that allows you to listen to music while studying.

## Getting Started

1. **Open the Widget**
   - Press `Ctrl+Shift+M` or go to **Tools ‚Üí ‚ô´ Music Player ‚Üí Toggle**

2. **Select Your Service**
   - Use the dropdown menu to choose YouTube Music, YouTube, or a custom URL
   - The default service is YouTube Music

3. **Log In**
   - Log in to your account if using YouTube Music/YouTube
   - Your session will be saved automatically for future use

4. **Control Playback**
   - Use the on-screen buttons or keyboard shortcuts to control music
   - The widget stays on top while you study
